Demographic Model of the Italian population from 2002 to 2100

Visualization of my model of the Italian population based on the data published by ISTAT (Italian National Institute of Statistics).

or checking the model, calculation by calculation, visit my GitHub repository Saturday-Morning-Projects, and don't hesitate to contribute!

Description of the Model

Presenting the 3x3 scenarios

For the fertility I assume 3 scenarios: lowering to 0 in 2100, constant, and increasing to 2 in 2100

For the net migration balance I assume 3 scenarios: null migration since this year, constant (average since 2002), increasing to 5 times the current one in 2100.

Extrapolating the future the Population Growth Rate for the older population
